# Apakose Ezekiel Imoleayo — AI Search Specialist & Yoruba Language Data Researcher

I work at the exact point where African linguistics meets AI systems. My work runs in two directions that connect at the same root problem: Nigerian businesses are invisible to AI-powered search, and the AI systems doing the searching were never trained on the language those businesses operate in.

For Nigerian businesses, I provide SEO, AEO, and GEO services built for the way AI platforms — Perplexity, ChatGPT Search, Google AI Overviews — now surface information. For AI companies and researchers, I am building a primary-source Yoruba language corpus through structured elder interviews in Lagos: spoken vocabulary, proverbs, tonal patterns, and cultural context that no web scrape will ever capture.
